# Data-Analytics-For-Palm-Oil-Supply-Chain

This school project report is for educational purposes. It involves the design and implementation of four interactive dashboard using Tableau Desktop, aimed at exploring datasets associated with the palm oil industry. SawitPRO’s mission is to revolutionize the palm oil industry by enhancing productivity, sustainability, and collaboration among stakeholders in the palm oil supply chain therefore, through this assignment, I have cleaned, prepared, and analyzed the data to generate insightful visualizations and dashboards that support SawitPRO’s business objectives. By addressing all my exploratory question, I aim to provide valuable insights for SawitPRO to improve decision-making, optimize operations, and identify growth opportunities within the palm oil industry.
